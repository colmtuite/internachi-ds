InterNACHI Online Home Inspector Exam

This exam is completely free and open to everyone. You can take it as many times as you like. Passing this exam is one of the InterNACHI entrance requirements.

Go to exam

Exam overview

Questions are randomly generated from a constantly evolving pool of questions. You can take the exam as often as you like. Each time you take the exam it will be completely unique.

  • Total questions: 120
  • Exam time: 1 hour
  • Pass mark: 80

Scoring breakdown

The exam uses a weighted scoring system. For example, questions which have safety implications score more marks than questions which have less serious implications. The more important the question, the more marks will be deducted for answering incorrectly. However, extra marks will not necessarily be awarded for correctly answering important questions.

Inversely, extra marks will not be rewarded for correctly answering easy questions but extra marks may be deducted for failing to answer easy questions.
